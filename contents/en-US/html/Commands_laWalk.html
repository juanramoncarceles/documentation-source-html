Walk mode

Keyboard:
laWalk
Icon:
left click
Toolbar:Lands Tools
Menu:
Lands Design > Walk Mode

The Walk mode command lets you walk through and fly freely over the project using the keyboard and mouse buttons.

After running the laWalk command click the SPACE button to switch from Walk Mode to Fly Mode and vice versa:

  • Walk Mode: you move through the project detecting the terrain elevation and other lands elements.
  • Fly Mode: you move freely in any direction and elevation over the project.

Walk/Fly Mode Controllers

Mouse

  • Pointer: controls the direction to look at.
  • Long left click: walks forward.
  • Long right click: walks backward.
  • Wheel: increases/decreases the walking or flying speed.
  • Center button: sets the default speed.

Keyboard

  • SPACE: switches from Walk Mode to Fly Mode and vice versa.
  • A: moves the walker to the left.
  • D: moves the walker to the right.
  • W: walks forward.
  • S: walks backward.
  • + and -: control the walker's height.
