Identify Plants

Keyboard:
laPlantLabelSelectPlants
Icon:
left click
Toolbar: Lands Plant Label
Menu:
Lands Design > Documentation > Plant Label > Identify Plants

The laPlantLabelSelectPlants command helps you identify the plants linked to a Plant label object.

Steps:

  1. Select the Plant label whose plants you want to identify. Right-click or hit ENTER when done.

The plants linked to the label object will be highlighted.
