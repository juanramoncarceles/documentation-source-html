Plant Label Commands


Plant Label commands provide labelling functions to plant species with identification code and specify the quantity of specimens exiting in the model.

Plant specimens can be labelled individually or as a linking set.

Plant Label Parameters

General

General attributes for the plant label: Name, Color, Layer, and Transparency (only visible in Conceptual display mode).

Label

This tab defines the label and the text format:

  • Shape: parameters of how the information is displayed in the label and border format.
  • Font: text attributes and parameters.
  • Leader: when this button is enabled, a new tab appears with the leader settings.


Label tab.


Leader

Leader settings. This tab only appears if the Leader checkbox is enabled in the Label tab:

  • Line: parameters of the leader line
  • End: parameters of the leader pointer


Leader tab.
