Identify Label (Tag)

Keyboard:
laPlantSelectTag
Icon:
left click
Toolbar:
Menu:
Lands Design > Documentation > Tag Plants > Identify Label

The laPlantSelectTag command helps you identify the tag object from a selection of plant species.

Steps:

  1. Select the plant or plants species whose tag you want to identify. Right-click or hit ENTER when done.

The tag object will be highlighted.
