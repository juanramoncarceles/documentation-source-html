Identify Label

Where can I find this command?
Keyboard:
laPlantSelectLabel
Icon:
left click [long click]
Toolbar: Lands Plant Label
Menu:
Lands Design > Documentation > Plant Label > Identify Label
Edit Panel:
Select a plant > Edit area in Edit Panel > Plant > Identify Label
Contextual menu:
Select a plant > Plant > Identify Label

The laPlantSelectLabel command identifies the label that is showing the information of a plant species and highlights it.

A Plant label is required to identify it, otherwise no object will be highlighted.

Steps:

  1. Select the Plant species whose label you want to identify.
