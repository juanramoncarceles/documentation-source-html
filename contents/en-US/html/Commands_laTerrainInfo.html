Terrain Info

Where can I find this command?
Keyboard:
laTerrainInfo
Icon:
left click
Terrain: Lands Terrain
Menu:
Lands Design > Terrain Modeling > Terrain > Volume of Earthmoving
Edit Panel:
Select a terrain > Edit area in Edit Panel > Terrain > Volume of Earthmoving

This commands opens a dialog that informs you about the total amount of volume from cuts and fills applied on a terrain.

Steps:

  1. Pick a terrain. Press Enter or right-click when done.


Terrain Information dialog.
