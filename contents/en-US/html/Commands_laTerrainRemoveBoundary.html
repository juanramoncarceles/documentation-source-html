Terrain Remove Boundary

Where can I find this command?
Keyboard:
laTerrainRemoveBoundary
Menu:
Lands Design > Terrain Modeling > Terrain > Remove Boundary
Edit Panel:
Select a terrain > Edit area in Edit Panel > Terrain > Remove boundary

This command removes from a terrain a boundary that has been previously added with the laTerrainBoundary command.

Steps:

  1. Pick a terrain. Press Enter or right-click when done.

The terrain will recover its original boundary.


Terrain after defining a new boundary.

The boundary can be removed also from the Terrain Edit area of the Edit panel and Properties dialog, under the Input data tab, after selecting the terrain.
